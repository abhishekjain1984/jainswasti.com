About

Through my work, I want to communicate the awe I have for life force. Be it decades of joys or struggles in one’s life being reflected in the ever-expressive face or a fleeting bloom of a flower, I want my work to express it. I hope by capturing an expression or a bloom, I can provide the viewer a calm escape, a reason to pause and appreciate the mysteries of life.
